Yumerang was a television companion app designed to deliver meme-style content as a second-screen experience while watching TV. Our core challenge was optimizing the flow of information and determining how best to surface the most relevant shows based on user preferences.

We considered two approaches:

Prompt users to complete an online questionnaire outlining their TV interests.

Automatically push trending content and learn user preferences based on in-app behavior—favoring shows according to likes and dislikes.

Ultimately, we chose the second path: letting user interactions guide personalized content delivery. This approach allowed us to continuously refine recommendations in real time, providing a more seamless and engaging user experience.
